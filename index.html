About Me

I'm Victoria Mwende, a passionate Software Engineering student born in the beautiful Meru County. Currently in my second year at Kirinyaga University, I'm on a journey to master the art of creating impactful technology solutions.

Growing up in Meru instilled in me values of hard work, community, and innovation. I believe technology should be accessible, inclusive, and designed to solve real-world problems, especially for communities like ours.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Kenyan tech communities, mentoring younger students in STEM, or enjoying our beautiful Kenyan landscapes. I'm particularly interested in how technology can be leveraged for agricultural innovation and women's empowerment in tech.
